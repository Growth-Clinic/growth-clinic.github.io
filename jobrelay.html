Getting the Most from JobRelay

Follow these tips to maximize your job search success with JobRelay

Setting Up JobRelay

  1. Install the extension from the Firefox Add-ons Store
  2. Click the JobRelay icon in your browser toolbar to open the dashboard
  3. Set up your profile first (resume data and career goals)
  4. Explore the tools - Search, AI Prompts, and Application Tracking

Pro Tip

Complete your profile 100% to unlock all features of JobRelay. This includes your personal info, experience, skills, and career goals.

Setting Up Your Resume

  1. Import with AI - Upload your resume to ChatGPT/Claude using our prompt
  2. Paste the JSON into the import field
  3. Click "Import from JSON" to load your resume data
  4. Important: Click "Save Resume Data" after reviewing or making changes

Common Mistake

Many users forget to click "Save Resume Data" after importing their CV. Always save after importing or making changes!

Defining Your Career Goals

  1. Navigate to the Profile tab and find the Career Goals section
  2. Select your transition type - new job, role change, industry change, etc.
  3. Enter your target roles - be specific about positions you're seeking
  4. Enter your target locations - list cities or regions of interest
  5. Important: Click "Save Career Goals" to store your preferences

Why This Matters

Your career goals personalize the Search Tool and AI Prompts features, making them much more effective for your specific situation.

Using the Search Tool

  1. Navigate to the Search Tool tab
  2. Click "Load from Career Goals" to automatically fill your preferences
  3. Adjust any specific parameters as needed
  4. Click "Generate Search Formulas" to create powerful search queries
  5. Copy or click "Open" to use these formulas directly

Hidden Job Tip

The "Company Career Pages" search is particularly effective for finding jobs that aren't posted on major job boards!

Getting Perfect AI Prompts

  1. Navigate to the AI Prompts tab
  2. Load a job description from a current page or saved application
  3. Important: Click the refresh button (↻) to update the prompt with your latest profile data
  4. Select the prompt type - Transferable Skills, CV Tailoring, etc.
  5. Copy the generated prompt and paste it into ChatGPT or Claude

Remember

Always click the refresh button (↻) after updating your profile or when switching between prompt types to ensure the latest data is incorporated!
