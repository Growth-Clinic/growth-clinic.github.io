The Job Success Framework: 8-Week Journey

Each week tackles a specific job search challenge and builds the skills to overcome it

Week 1: Career Targeting

Pain Point: "I don't know exactly what job I should be looking for."

Goal: Define your ideal role with precision, creating a clear target for your search efforts.

Week 2: Skill Mapping

Pain Point: "I'm not sure if I have the right skills for the jobs I want."

Goal: Create a comprehensive inventory of your current skills and identify gaps that need to be filled.

Week 3: Experience Translation

Pain Point: "My background doesn't seem relevant to the roles I'm targeting."

Goal: Identify valuable skills from all your experiences that transfer to your target role.

Week 4: Professional Packaging

Pain Point: "My CV and online presence aren't getting responses."

Goal: Develop compelling professional materials that showcase your value.

Week 5: Opportunity Discovery

Pain Point: "I can't find good job openings, or there's too much competition."

Goal: Develop strategies to find opportunities that others miss.

Week 6: Strategic Outreach

Pain Point: "My applications disappear into the void with no response."

Goal: Implement strategies that break through the noise and get noticed.

Week 7: Interview Mastery

Pain Point: "I get nervous in interviews and don't present my best self."

Goal: Develop the skills and confidence to excel in any interview format.

Week 8: Offer Optimization

Pain Point: "I don't know how to negotiate or set myself up for success."

Goal: Secure the best possible offer and create a plan for early success.
