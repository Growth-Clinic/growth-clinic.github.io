The Founder Experience

Join us in the Growth Clinic to co-own and build your venture.
Experience the journey firsthand.

  • Pick a problem from the challenges and opportunities we face locally
  • Ideate and test a solution using proven frameworks and feedback from real-world users
  • Develop the solution into a minimal MVP with its own business model that brings in paying customers
  • Coming soon: We invest in you and help you source further investment

  • Starts at ₦30,000 for the first 12 weeks. Then ₦3,000 monthly

The Onboarding Experience

1

You apply to join the Founder Program

2

Your application is reviewed. Once accepted, you'll be scheduled for a video interview

3

If successful, you'll be sent a link to make the initial payment.

4

After payment, You will be redirected to our online learning platform where you get all you need to start your founder experience

5

First 12 weeks: You'll pick and develop the ideas into business-ready solutions. You'll test and experiment to design a business model and develop the initial MVP. You'll learn the basics of practical business growth.

6

Second 12 weeks and after: You'll continue developing the MVP and the business model to bring in customers. Building the product into a business that creates a new source of income for you.

How Payment Works

You pay with time and money. A minimum of 10 hours a week is required to be successful with the program. To help us improve the program and manage the community, you pay the one-time fee of ₦30,000 for the first 12 weeks, after that, you pay ₦3,000 monthly.

You get shared ownership in the business you build and learn community and product led growth, thereby creating a new source of income for yourself.
