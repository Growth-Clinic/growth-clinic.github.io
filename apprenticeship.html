Growth Clinic Apprenticeship Programme

A 3-month structured learning and contribution opportunity where you'll receive
personalised training in mental models while building your professional portfolio.

Programme Overview

The Growth Clinic Apprenticeship Programme is designed for ambitious professionals looking to develop practical skills in applying personalised frameworks to real-world challenges.

Direct Mentorship

Receive guidance from Growth Clinic experts in personalised mental models

Hands-on Experience

Apply frameworks to real career and business challenges

Portfolio Building

Create case studies and projects to showcase your expertise

Career Advancement

Priority consideration for future opportunities with Growth Clinic

Framework Access

Access to proprietary frameworks and tools normally only available to paid programme participants

Certificate of Completion

Receive documentation of your acquired skills and contributions

Your 3-Month Apprenticeship Journey

A structured, transformative experience designed to build your expertise in personalised frameworks

Month 1: Learning Phase

Weekly 1-hour training sessions covering core mental models

Self-paced learning assignments applying frameworks to your own challenges

Shadow experienced team members during client interactions

Weekly reflection exercises to personalise frameworks

Month 2: Application Phase

Apply learned frameworks to a specific Growth Clinic project

Receive detailed feedback and guidance on your implementation

Develop practical skills in adapting frameworks for different contexts

Create a case study for your professional portfolio

Month 3: Contribution Phase

Lead a defined micro-project aligned with business needs

Present results and lessons learned to the Growth Clinic team

Document your personal framework adaptations

Create transition plan for ongoing relationship with Growth Clinic

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a paid position?

No, this is an unpaid apprenticeship focused on learning and skill development. However, you'll receive access to frameworks and tools valued at â‚¦75,000 and opportunities to earn commissions through referrals.

How flexible is the time commitment?

The programme requires 5-10 hours weekly, which can be scheduled flexibly around your other commitments. There will be some fixed times for weekly check-ins, but most work can be completed on your own schedule.

What happens after the 3-month programme?

Successful apprentices may be offered paid project work, affiliate opportunities, or priority consideration for future roles with Growth Clinic. All apprentices will have continued access to the community and resources.

What kind of projects will I work on?

Projects vary based on business needs and your skills, but may include content creation, framework development, community engagement initiatives, or programme enhancement projects.

Do I need specific qualifications to apply?

While we don't require specific qualifications, successful applicants typically have strong communication skills, intellectual curiosity, self-motivation, and interest in personal development and framework-based thinking.
